Job Title PRN-Occupational Therapist.0
Department Name Occupational Therapy
Job Requirements

POSITION SUMMARY:

Organizes and conducts Occupational Therapy programs to facilitate rehabilitation of the mentally or physically disabled. Directs patient participation in selected tasks to restore, reinforce, and enhance physical performance, facilitates learning of those skills and functions essential for adaptation and productivity, diminishes or corrects pathology, and promotes and maintains health. Assembles and reports data as necessary to the Director of Rehab Services for acquisition of capitol equipment, continuing education, annual operating budget, and quality assurance items.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • Licensed as an Occupational Therapist required.  
  • CPR (HCP) to be obtained with in 30 days of hire.  
  • Falls Prevention, OSHA, Restraints are to be obtained with in 30 days and renewable annually.
Status, Shifts and Hours Worked
PRN; weekend rotation; wekkday rehab coverage; flexible schedule.
