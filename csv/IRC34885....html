Detailed Description

·         Review and process orders entered during Asian or European market hours.

·         Review and process orders exceeding principal limits for Asian & European markets trading.

·         Review and resolve trading inquiries for Asian & European markets trading.

·         Provide Superior Customer/Trade Support to E*TRADE’s Customer Service Teams & Global Market Center Trade Support Teams

·         Receive inbound/make outbound calls to customer service as needed

·         Receive inbound/make outbound calls to GMT market centers as needed.

·         Assist Global Trading Supervisor with daily duties:

·         Provide supervisor with input related to process improvement.

·         Monitor and assist colleagues’ with their production levels.

·         Monitor and evaluate system performance.

·         Serve in temporary leadership role during absence of supervisor and or manager.

·         Resolve intradepartmental escalations utilizing independent decision making.

·         Provide technical expertise relating to E*TRADE’s trading systems.

·         Reconcile and audit GMT trading error accounts.

·         Identify areas in need of improvement and create workable solutions to problems.

·         Develop training materials and coordinate programs as needed on an ongoing basis.

·         Ability to communicate and work with various departments to improve procedures and minimize risk to the firm.

·         Work Asian Market hours, including US holidays.
