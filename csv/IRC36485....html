Detailed Description

Overview
E*TRADE Corporate Services provides equity compensation services to over 1500 corporate clients and over 1 million plan participants.  The Product Communications Segment Leader will report directly to the head of Product Management and is responsible creating, developing, and maintaining the client portal product communications strategy for E*TRADE Corporate Services.  This role is seeking an experienced and seasoned individual who has demonstrated long term product communications management or leadership.


Primary Responsibilities

·         Define and manage the content strategy, requirements, resources, applications and delivery of information for the E*TRADE Corporate Services online client portal

·         Aggressively seek communications content newsworthy for corporate clients by working with senior industry consultants and experts

·         Identify the strategic value added content channels that can be promoted to the client portal site on a daily basis

·         Create and write new equity compensations whitepapers, articles, or presentations that are topical and timely and will bring value to corporate clients

·         Manage the content strategy and communications placement of training, marketing, and client related messaging on the client portal

·         Actively participate as an industry expert and leading voice in the marketplace by building strong relationships with third party firms

·         Drive the strategic direction of the corporate client online community, including facilitation of relevant and topical product and industry information. 

·         Perform website maintenance, including the review of all content for currency, accuracy, organization and other requirements and recommend technology improvements.

·         Work with subject matter experts and business owners to ensure E*TRADE Corporate Services online client portal content is current, accurate and effective.
Job Requirements

Qualifications

·         Minimum of 7-10 years related experience in Product Marketing, Product Communications, and/or website content management.  Equity compensation, benefits, or financial services knowledge is preferred

·         Track record of managing large scale client communications or marketing projects

·         Demonstrate success of driving the strategy for multiple communication efforts at one time

·         Must be strong writer with excellent English grammar and communication skills

·         Possess strong organizational and analytical skills; have experience in website content development and management

·         Project Management experience; experience selecting, implementing and maintaining Content Management Systems helpful

·         Must be extremely detail-oriented and be able to manage numerous projects at one time

·         Strong computer skills including Excel, Word, Access, and PowerPoint

·         Bachelor degree or equivalent education

·         Certified Equity Professional level 1, 2, or 3 preferred
