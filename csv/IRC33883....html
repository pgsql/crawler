Detailed Description

-  Provide support to employees at all organizational levels concerning general benefits and other inquiries regarding HR policies, procedures, and programs.

-  Collaborate with the HR Business Partner in handling employee relations issues and addressing performance improvement plans.

-  Possess a strong knowledge of employee benefit programs including without limitation:  group medical, dental & vision insurances; STD & LTD, voluntary life, flex spending, 401(k), stock option grants.

-  Present and facilitate New Hire Orientation as per schedule.

-  Conduct all exit meetings for the site while communicating findings to HR management.

-  Work with HRIS and the HR Business Partner team to ensure data is accurate in the Oracle system.  Enter Oracle changes as needed.

-  Run monthly and weekly ad hoc reports from Oracle HRIS system when needed.

-  Coordinate all paperwork for new hires, status changes and terminations (collection of new hire paperwork, ETAFs and termination packets).

-  Create, file and maintain employee and benefit files or the Arlington location.

-  Collaborate with and support HR Business Partners and Management on projects.

-  Other duties and responsibilities as assigned. 

Job Requirements

-  Bachelor’s Degree (B.A/B.S) or equivalent

-  5 + years Corporate Human Resources experience.

-  Ability to prioritize tasks and work effectively independently and as a member of a team

-  Exercise sound judgment and discretion in maintaining all confidential information

-  Knowledge of employment laws and regulations with prior experience in applying to the workplace.

-  Strong organizational, problem-solving, interpersonal and analytical skills.

-  Excellent communication and presentation skills.

-  Ability to handle multiple projects and meet deadlines.

-  Must be proficient in Microsoft Office

-  Knowledge of Oracle HRIS system a plus
