Detailed Description

POSITION SUMMARY/PURPOSE OF POSITION:

Reporting directly to the Manager, Corporate Security, the Corporate Security Administrator interacts directly with E*TRADE business units at assigned locations regarding physical safety and security issues.  Performs duties in compliance with company and department security policies and procedures across assigned locations.  Duties include but are not limited to:  meet and greet customers, visitors, contractors, vendors; transfers incoming calls as needed; issues temporary badges; fingerprints new hires; creating photo badges for new employees in conjunction with the operations center;  monitors security cameras and systems; dispatches vendors during malfunctions; prevent unauthorized entry by intruders and other security violations; investigates and document reports of theft, vandalism, and other security related incidents; provides metrics and reports to management as needed;  responds to reports of employees in medical distress; summon emergency responders and law enforcement as needed; participates in emergency preparation teams; performs quarterly and annual account audit requirements as assigned; assists management in working with guard services for assigned locations. Helps identify risk areas, recommends preventive solutions, and implements correction action plans as appropriate to ensure safety of employees and protection of company assets.  Takes a lead role in ensuring documentation is kept current and accurately reflects the environment they support.  Training and mentor CSA staff and contingent workers as needed to ensure incessant operational consistency and efficiency.
