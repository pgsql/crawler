Detailed Description

Ensure accurate and timely processing of all regulatory required tax related filings and notices. Process tax related inquiries and potential errors as received, from customers, E*TRADE Financial personnel, financial institutions, and government agencies requests. Identify, document, and resolve exceptions and communicate with internal departments such as Customer Service, Fraud, Operations, and Compliance.

Key focus:

Work with appropriate internal and external business partners to validate and disseminate tax documents as required.

- Ensure accuracy and timeliness of all Federal and State tax filing as required.

- Review customer related documents for completeness and regulatory compliance.

- Respond to related Operations and Customer Service inquiries within specified service levels.

- Develop test plans and scripts to ensure proper validation of regulatory changes and business enhancements.

- Interpret Federal and State regulatory changes into the development of business requirements for technical changes and process enhancements.

- Consistently meet or exceed established quality and productivity goals.

- Assist with retirement and estate account processing as needed.

- Maintain knowledge of E*TRADE policies and procedures.

- Effectively communicate any business environment

issues or concerns to management.

- Perform other duties as required.
Job Requirements

Minimum Required Skills:

- Minimum of 3 years experience at a financial services firm handling Federal and State tax and retirement related processing.

- Strong knowledge of regulations governing rules for 1099s, 1042s, 990Ts, 2439's and back up withholding processing.

- Must be a dynamic and self-starting individual, able to work as part of a team or independently, with strong organizational skills.

- Strong prioritization skills and ability to adapt to change.

- Strong organizational skills and prioritization techniques to adapt to changing priorities

- Strong analytical and problem solving skills

- Excellent verbal and written communication skills
