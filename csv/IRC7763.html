Job Title Radiation Therapist
Department Name Cyberknife
Job Requirements

POSITION SUMMARY:

Administers a prescribed, planned, therapeutic dose of ionizing radiation to patients undergoing CyberKnife Therapy according to departmental policy and procedure.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • Require graduate of an accredited Radiation Therapy program.
  • Current Louisiana license in the field of Radiation Therapy required. 
  • Must be registered with A.R.R.T. or registry eligible in the field of Radiation Therapy.  
  • Experience of 3 years as a Radiation Therapist is preferred.
