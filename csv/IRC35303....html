Detailed Description

The supervisor of the Mutual Funds Trading and Operations department will be responsible for overseeing incoming order flow and managing the relationship between the E*TRADE CLEARING LLC’s middle office & clearing partner (Ridge Clearing & Outsourcing Solutions Inc) and between E*TRADE CLEARING LLC’S middle office & E*TRADE SECURITIES LLC’s Service department. 

Job Requirements

Daily processes will include, but is not limited to:

·         Review & release of mutual fund orders

·         Reconcile and communicate rejected trades

·         Service customer requests

·         Control and oversight of suspense and error accounts

·         Comply with firm policies and SOX controls.

 

The  ideal candidate should demonstrate the ability to work with diverse areas of the firm to help resolve issues relating to trade rejects, broker errors, aged suspense positions or balances,  ET proprietary system updates and workflow resolutions. 

 

He/she should also be able to multi-task in helping resolve high exposure items, monitor system performance and effectively communicate with senior-management and clearing partner.

 

This supervisory position also requires:

·         The resolution of intradepartmental escalations, utilizing independent decision making processes

·         A willingness to work additional hours as necessary in response to increased workload

·         The ability to provide technical and regulatory expertise relating to E*Trade’s middle and back office operating systems

·         Identification of process inefficiencies and provide solutions.

·         Participation in firm projects

·         The management of a team of E*TRADE CLEARING LLC’S mutual fund associates as well as an outsourced team.
