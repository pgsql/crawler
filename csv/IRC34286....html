Detailed Description

Position Summary:  We are looking for a seasoned Product Manager with experience developing and implementing enterprise CRM/Sales Automation solutions using Salesforce.com or other equivalent platform.  Successful candidate will possess strong management, communications, leadership, and problem-solving skills. Desired candidate is someone who thrives in a team-oriented, results-oriented, and customer-focused environment, welcomes challenge, embraces change, is creative, can learn fast, and works well under tight deadlines. 

 

This Senior Product Manager will guide the development, implementation and ongoing product management of all Enterprise CRM and Sales Automation applications.

 

Duties & Responsibilities:  Candidate must be able to work with senior management, assess customer service needs, effectively communicate with sales force, identify cost saving opportunities, manage product lifecycles, define detailed business and product specifications, and work within a cross-functional team (technology, operations, customer service, sales, marketing, and business) to manage an end-to-end rollout of projects. 

Candidate must be highly motivated, extremely organized, detail-oriented and possess outstanding presentation, communication and customer service skills.   

 

Key responsibilities for this job are:

§         Work as an Advance Administrator to design, build, and launch new Salesforce.com applications as well as enhance existing applications based on business requirements

§         Evaluate enterprise CRM needs, analyze and evaluate the current applications, and externally available solutions to define and execute an effective CRM solutions roadmap.

§         Define business and product requirements to enhance and build the E*TRADE CRM platform leveraging Salesforce.com.  Requirements must address the needs of customer service, trading, operations, marketing and sales.

§         Write business and functional requirements necessary to develop new work tools and CRM solutions or enhance existing programs to improve the customer service experience, ContactCenter performance, workforce management, operational efficiencies, and the tracking and reporting of key metrics.

§         Work closely with technology to design the solutions needed, and to manage rollout of products and tools in a planned and timely manner.

§         Identify necessary reports, metrics, and analysis to measure the effectiveness and validate the success of projects completed.

§         Provide project management for the development, testing and launch of new products and enhancements to existing product and solutions.
