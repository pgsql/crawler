Detailed Description
  •  Assist HR Manager in handling employee relations issues and  addressing performance improvement plans; provide coaching to employees and management staff.   
  •  Address employees’ inquiries regarding company policies, procedures and HR Programs.

  •  Administer the Contingent Workforce Program.
  • Perform Oracle HRIS data maintenance, including: new hires, terminations, and process status changes for employees and contingent workforce.   Generate monthly and adhoc reports when needed. Work with HRIS to ensure accurate data is in the Oracle system. 
  • Facilitate New Hire Orientations and process all new hire and termination paperwork.  Conduct exit meetings and communicating findings to HR management.
  • Provide Leave of Absences support – FMLA, CFRA, Personal Leave, and Military Leave.  Coordinate and monitor all leaves of absences (LOAs) from initial request through final return to work. Work closely with Payroll and Benefits Team to administer and resolve any issues as they occur. 
  • Administer Worker’s Compensation program: filing claims with carrier and assist employee with claim filing from initial request through final return to work. Conduct ergo evaluations, and work with facilities staff, to ensure workstations are properly set-up and provide any necessary equipment.
  • Create, file, and maintaining employee’s personnel files. 

  • Other duties and responsibilities as assigned
Job Requirements
  • BS degree and 3+ years of Human Resources experience.  Previous Oracle HRIS experience is preferred.

  • Working knowledge of California state and federal employment laws and regulations.

  • Exercise sound judgment and discretion in maintain all confidential information.

  • Excellent communication skills to effectively interact with employees and management.

  • Ability to prioritize tasks and work independently with minimal supervision.

  • Ability to work in a fast-paced work environment and meet deadlines.

  • Must be proficient in Word, Excel, Power Point
